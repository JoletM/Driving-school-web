Adult Driving Class

Adult 6 Hour Class

Covers basic vehicle controls, traffic rules, and safe driving habits. The classes are every 3rd Saturday of the month from 8:00am–2:30pm. Includes the written exam for either your permit or road test at DPS. The Texas Drivers Education Certificate will be given to you once the class is finished and you have passed the written exam. The course is $120.00 CASH ONLY. If you would like to sign up for the adult course, please come in person to sign a contract, basic information, and bring payment. We will NOT ask for any paperwork.

Road Test

The road test with us is $120 CASH ONLY and it is a one chance only. If you do not do well your first time and would like to try again, it will be another $120. Please read the information down below to know what to bring, you can always call us and ask as well. If you need to rent one of our cars it is $50.00 EXTRA.

Available Road Test Dates

  • Monday-Saturdays
  • M-F: 12:00pm and latest is 4:30pm
  • Saturday: 11:30am-1:00pm
  • What you need for a road test: Permit (up to date), Impact Texas Drivers Certificate (up to date), if under 25 years old you will need proof that you took a course (either 6 hour class or teen), and car insurance if you would like to use your own car.
  • Please call and make an appointment for the road test
  • If you are PARENT TAUGHT you will need to bring these as extra: All driving logs and classroom hour log (if given) so we can double check your paperwork

Teen Course

For teens 15 and up, we accept until 21. The course includes 24 hours of classroom time with the permit test included and 14 hours with the instructor (7 observing and 7 driving). Classes run Monday–Thursday from 6–8 PM. The course is $460. You may pay with either card or cash. If paying by card, there is a $10 fee. If paying by cash, it must be exact. If you would like to sign up please come in person to fill out a contract, basic information, and bring payment. We will NOT ask for any paperwork.
